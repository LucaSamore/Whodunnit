## Review
All activities planned for this sprint were successfully completed. The implementation and testing of case generation were completed. Significant progress was made on the Versioning feature with the completion of its logical design.

## Retrospective

Finalizing the case generation feature (FR1) removes a significant dependency and allows the team to shift focus to other modules. 
The early implementation of the CD pipeline consolidates the development infrastructure, which will optimize future deployments. 
The workload distribution in this sprint was effective and resulted in the completion of all set objectives.
